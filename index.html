About Us

The Legends Barbershop is a professional hair cutting establishment that was opened in 2006 in scenic downtown New Braunfels, Texas, with the mission of providing the availability of excellence in barbering exclusively for men. The Fellas concept is simple, offer a unique, quality, and timeless tonsorial barber experience at very competitive prices.

The Legends Barbershop employes four professionals that have gathered over 100 years of combined experience in the barber trade. Each individual offers a particular benefit to the shop with their personal contribution and expertise. Be it shop ownership, military training, or just precision hair cutting, the staff at the Legends Barbershop will make you feel at home and very satisfied with the way you look.

The Legends Barbershop brings back that old time barber shop feel; hot lather, hot towel, and electric shoulder massage after every haircut. Truly a haircut experience like no other.
